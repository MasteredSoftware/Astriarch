About: Astriarch - Ruler of the Stars



Overview

Astriarch - Ruler of the Stars is a Turn-based, Single Player Space Strategy Game. Build planetary improvements and star ships to capture planets and defeat your enemies. Your ultimate goal is to become the master of the known universe, and earn the title of Astriarch!


Background

Developed in 2010 by Mastered Software, Astriarch combines aspects of other classic space strategy games such as Master of Orion 2 (MOO2), Stellar Conflict (1987 Amiga), and Star Control.

Astriarch is a free casual web game.  The game will continue to be improved to increase playability and maximize fun while still staying simple and easy to learn.

The name Astriarch comes from the Ancient Greek words for star (ástron) and ruler (arkhos).


Credits

Astriarch - Ruler of the Stars, space strategy game designed and developed by Mastered Software, music by Resonant. Astriarch uses Stratiscape JavaScript Canvas Library and the jQuery Listbox plugin.


Open Source

Astriarch source code has been released on GitHub. The open source code is intended to provide an example of how to create a turn-based strategy game in using HTML5, JavaScript and NodeJS.
