Help: Astriarch - Ruler of the Stars



  • Each Farm increases the amount of Food produced for one farmer.
  • Each Mine increases the amount of Ore and Iridium produced for one miner.
  • Gold is generated by taxing the population, the higher the total population, the more gold generated each turn. Gold is also generated when builders are assigned to a planet with an empty build queue.
  • Factories allow construction of Space Platforms as well as increase the production generated for one worker.
  • Space Platforms provide planetary defense and allow cruiser and battleship construction.
  • Colonies increase the planet's maximum population.
  • At the end of each turn damaged StarShips will be repaired given the population is not rioting or in unrest and there is a Colony on the planet as well as the structure required to build the StarShip. Resources are required for StarShip repairs as well.
  • Defenders protect a planet from attacking fleets but cannot move between planets.
  • Scouts, destroyers, cruisers and battleships can attack other planets and their respective strengths increase exponentially.
  • Shipping one food between planets costs one gold
  • Starvation slows planetary production and population growth
  • Population grows based on existing population and space available on the planet (more space and higher population means faster growth)
  • Citizens will protest new rulers on a planet. Protesting citizens cannot be used as workers and will calm down each turn given they have enough food. Loyalty will shift to the new planet owner once a citizen stops protesting. If more than half of the population is protesting, the overall population will be in unrest and production will be reduced.
  • A player's home (starting) planet gives advantages: more gold generated through taxes, starvation is less likely to cause unrest, and protesting citizens become content more quickly.

Starship Advantages and Disadvantages


  • Some starships have natural combat advantages over other starships.
  • Advantages increase attack strength by 50%, disadvantages decrease attack strength by 50%.
  • Space Platforms have advantage over all ships
  • Battleships -> Cruisers -> Destroyers -> Scouts -> Defenders (-> Battleships) *
* (-> means has an advantage over)

Research and Development

  • Players can design custom ships or research improvements to star ship effectiveness, building efficiency, etc...
  • Tax revenue can be directed to Research and Development Projects.
  • Each new level attained in a research project improves the effectiveness of the researched area and subsequent levels become more difficult to achieve.
  • Some research projects are inherently more difficult to research than others.
  • During successful planetary conflicts, the attacking fleet has a chance to discover research points, if this results in a level up in a research area, then attacking and defending players are notified.


Galactic Trading Center

  • Players may sell or buy food, ore, and iridium at the Galactic Trading center.
  • Trades are processed in the order they are submitted.
  • Prices fluctuate based on the resource amounts held by the trading center.
  • Buy trades may fail to execute if the trading center runs out of a resource or the player runs out of gold.
  • Sell trades may fail to execute if the player runs out of a resource or the trading center runs out of gold.
